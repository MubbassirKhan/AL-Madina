Spoken Arabic Course

Course Overview

Enhance your conversational skills in Arabic through structured lessons and immersive practice sessions. Our Spoken Arabic course is designed for learners at all levels, enabling them to communicate effectively in various everyday situations.

What You Will Learn

  • Basic conversational phrases and vocabulary
  • Pronunciation and accent improvement
  • Role-playing real-life scenarios
  • Listening and comprehension skills
  • Cultural nuances and etiquette in conversations

Course Duration and Fees

Duration: 3 Months (12 Weeks)

Fees: $300

Upon successful completion, a certificate will be awarded to participants.
